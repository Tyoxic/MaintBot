MaintBot Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

Overview

MaintBot is a vehicle maintenance tracking app for Android and iOS. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how MaintBot handles your data.

Data Collection

MaintBot does not automatically collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

In App Store terms: MaintBot declares no data collection in its App Privacy nutrition label. No analytics SDK, no crash reporter, no advertising identifier, no user identifier.

All data you enter into MaintBot (vehicle information, maintenance logs, ride logs, notes, and profile details) is stored locally on your device using an on-device SQLite database. No data is sent to any server, cloud service, or third party.

Distribution

MaintBot is distributed two ways:

  • Android: APK files published on our GitHub Releases page. Users download and sideload the APK manually.
  • iOS: Via Apple's TestFlight beta program. Users install the TestFlight app and follow an invite link.

Neither distribution channel is used to collect data about you.

Data Storage

All app data is stored exclusively on your device. MaintBot does not use any remote servers, cloud storage, analytics services, or tracking tools. If you uninstall the app, all data is permanently deleted.

Data Export

MaintBot includes an optional export feature that lets you save your data as a JSON file to your device. This file is created locally and is only shared if you choose to share it yourself. MaintBot does not automatically upload or transmit this file.

Photos

MaintBot allows you to attach photos to vehicles from your device's photo library. These photos are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded or transmitted.

Notifications

MaintBot may send local notifications to remind you of upcoming registration expiry dates. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any external service.

Bug Reports

MaintBot includes an optional "Send Bug Report" button in the Profile screen. When you tap this button, the app prepares an email containing:

  • App version and update ID
  • Device platform (Android/iOS), OS version, and device name
  • Number of vehicles and maintenance log entries in your app (counts only — no vehicle names, hours, or personal details)
  • Recent JavaScript error messages from the current session, and (if available) from the previous session if the app crashed
  • A list of recent in-app actions and screens visited ("breadcrumbs") to help reproduce bugs — for example "Navigated to VehicleDetail"

To make these reports useful after a crash, MaintBot stores a small log file (error_log.json) in the app's private storage area on your device. This file contains only the error messages and breadcrumb entries described above — no vehicle data, hour readings, or personal details. The file is overwritten each session and stays on your device unless you send a bug report.

The email is opened in your device's email app. Nothing is sent automatically — you can review, edit, or cancel the message before sending. Bug reports are only collected when you choose to send one.

Third-Party Services

MaintBot does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services, advertising networks, or analytics platforms.

Children's Privacy

MaintBot does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us by opening an issue on our GitHub repository.
